About Me

I welcome adults and adolescents (ages 13+) confronting any number of concerns, including depression, anxiety, trauma, grief, identity questions, relationship difficulties, life transitions, and career issues. No matter what you are facing, I want you to feel safe, heard, and validated.


I hold an M.S.Ed. in Clinical Mental Health Counseling from Indiana University South Bend as well as a Ph.D. and M.A. in English (Literature) from the University of Notre Dame. I am also Geek Therapy Certified.


My pronouns are she/her.

Depth. Insight. Change.

We will work together to explore the root of your struggles, understand your thoughts, feelings, and behaviors, and create the change that you want.

Academia

After 20 years in higher education, I am well suited to work with individuals facing its unique challenges.

LGBTQIA+ Affirming

I am passionate about supporting people of all gender identities and affectional orientations.

Neurodivergent Affirming

I emphasize accepting all neurotypes, leveraging personal strengths, and changing environments rather than individuals.

Video Games

If games bring value to your life, I want to use them in our work together. If they are a problem, I want to help you to restore balance.
