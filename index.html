About Me

I welcome adults and adolescents confronting any number of concerns, including depression, anxiety, trauma, grief, identity questions, relationship difficulties, life transitions, and career issues. No matter what you are facing, I want you to feel safe, heard, and validated.


I hold an M.S.Ed. in Clinical Mental Health Counseling from Indiana University South Bend as well as a Ph.D. and M.A. in English (Literature) from the University of Notre Dame.

Depth. Insight. Change.

We will work together to explore the root of your struggles, understand your thoughts, feelings, and behaviors, and create the change that you want.

Academia

After 20 years in higher education, I am well suited to work with individuals facing its unique challenges.

LGBTQ+ Affirming

I am passionate about supporting people of all gender identities and affectional orientations.

Video Games
